How it works

From booking to WhatsApp report
in hours, not days.

The Hemato platform handles every step — patient, phlebotomist, and lab — in one connected workflow.

01
📱
Patient Books
Search tests, compare prices across nearby partner labs, pick a home collection slot or walk in. Pay via UPI, card, or cash.
02
🏍️
Phlebo Dispatched
Nearest certified phlebotomist is assigned, patient gets name, photo, and live ETA on WhatsApp. GPS-tracked throughout.
03
🧪
Sample Collected
Barcode printed, photo proof captured, cold-chain bag sealed. Sample transported to partner lab with temperature monitoring.
04
🔬
NABL Processing
9-step NABL/ISO 15189 compliant workflow inside Hemato LIS. Dual verification by technician and pathologist.
05
📄
Report Delivered
PDF report with H/L flags, clinical interpretations, and dual signatures sent directly to patient on WhatsApp. Stored in HealthBank.

The Hemato Advantage

1mg and PharmEasy broker orders — they have zero visibility inside the lab. Hemato controls quality, data, and the patient experience end-to-end.

10× Cheaper Than CrelioHealth
CrelioHealth charges ₹8,000–50,000/month. Hemato starts at free for basic and ₹999 for standard — enabling the 90,000 independent labs that were previously priced out of professional LIS software.
