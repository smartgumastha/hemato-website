How it works

Booking to WhatsApp report in hours.

Five steps, fully automated. Patient, phlebotomist, and lab in one connected workflow.

01
📱
Patient Books
Search tests, compare prices across nearby partner labs, pick home collection or walk-in, pay via UPI.
02
🏍️
Phlebo Dispatched
Nearest certified phlebotomist assigned. Patient gets name, photo, and live ETA on WhatsApp.
03
🧪
Sample Collected
Barcode printed, photo proof captured, cold-chain bag sealed. Temperature-monitored transit.
04
🔬
NABL Processing
9-step ISO 15189 compliant workflow in Hemato LIS. Dual verification by tech and pathologist.
05
📄
Report Delivered
PDF with H/L flags, clinical interpretation, dual signatures. Delivered on WhatsApp. Stored in HealthBank.

The Hemato Advantage

We own the lab's operating system.

1mg brokers orders. CrelioHealth sells LIS. Only Hemato does both — giving us end-to-end control over quality, data, and patient experience.

🔒
LIS Ownership = Unassailable Moat
Partner labs run their entire workflow on Hemato LIS daily. You can't unenroll from software your whole team depends on. Same lock-in strategy as Zoho for SMEs.
💰
10× Cheaper Than CrelioHealth
CrelioHealth charges ₹8,000–50,000/month. Hemato starts at free for basic and ₹999 for standard — serving the 90,000 labs that were priced out until now.
📱
WhatsApp-First for India
500M+ Indians on WhatsApp. Every OTP, report, booking confirmation, and phlebo update delivered there. No app download required for patients.
